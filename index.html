Our Story

Beauty born from passion & science

Founded in 2018, LUXÉRA began with a simple belief: everyone deserves cosmetics that are as kind to the planet as they are to your skin. We source the rarest botanicals — Moroccan argan oil, Japanese camellia extract, Bulgarian rose — and combine them with breakthrough formulations in our Paris laboratory.

Every product undergoes 47 quality checks before reaching your hands. We're proudly cruelty-free, vegan, and committed to zero-waste packaging by 2026. Our formulas are free from parabens, sulfates, and phthalates.
